Why Donate Blood?

Your blood donation is a lifeline for someone in need — from accident victims to cancer patients. Every drop counts and can make a difference between life and death.

Who Can Donate?

Healthy adults aged 17-65, weighing 50kg+, with no major health issues can become blood donors.

The Process

Simple health check, safe blood collection, and quick recovery with refreshments provided.

After Donation

Drink fluids, avoid heavy exercise, and feel proud knowing you've saved lives!
